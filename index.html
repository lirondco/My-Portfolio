    About Liron

    I moved to San Diego in 2013 upon graduation from university with a Bachelor's Degree in International Relations. Though my interest and passion are in electoral politics, languages, and music, my career developed by chance in the Hospitality Industry which gave me an opportunity to meet people from all over the world and from different walks of life.

    Though I've had prior exposure to coding and web development in high school, I started my journey in web development when the COVID19 pandemic struck the State of Israel and my then employer had to lay off employees due to the ensuing lockdown and subsequent economic downturn. Four months of unemployment gave me a lot of free time to study code and I did so through various resources on the web. Soon after, I decided to return to Southern California and I decided to become a thinkful student in order to have a better direction in my growth as a developer.
